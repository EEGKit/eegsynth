mode 6: dual color slider, segment can be moved along the array (between the edges)
  channel 1  = color 1 red
  channel 2  = color 1 green
  channel 3  = color 1 blue
  channel 4  = color 2 red
  channel 5  = color 2 green
  channel 6  = color 2 blue
  channel 7  = intensity
  channel 8  = position (from 0-255 or 0-360 degrees, relative to the length of the array)
  channel 9  = width    (from 0-255 or 0-360 degrees, relative to the length of the array)
